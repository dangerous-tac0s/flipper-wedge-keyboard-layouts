# Key differences:
# - Numbers require SHIFT (unshifted row has accented letters)
# - Z and Y are swapped
# - Unshifted: + ě š č ř ž ý á í é = ´
# - Shifted:   1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 0 % ˇ
